Why Flick

Everything else asks you to think like a crypto user.
Flick doesn't.

Existing wallets make you manage seed phrases, pick a chain, and hold gas tokens before you can send a cent. Flick collapses all of it into one gesture.

Send to an email

No wallet address, no ENS, no QR-scavenger-hunt. Type an email or phone, flick, done.

Gift-card UX

The recipient never sees "wallet" or "seed phrase." It feels like redeeming a gift card, not onboarding into DeFi.

Viral by design

Claiming a flick creates a wallet in seconds — and a shareable moment. Every recipient is a new sender.

Chain-abstracted

Balances across chains converge into one number. You never pick a network — Flick routes it.
